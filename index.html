About Dr. Chanchal Vagrecha

Dr. Chanchal Vagrecha is a dental professional with over 16 years of clinical experience, committed to helping patients achieve healthy teeth and confident smiles.

With a gentle and patient-centered approach, Dr. Vagrecha provides comprehensive dental care ranging from routine check-ups to restorative treatments, dental implants, and cosmetic procedures, with a focus on comfort, ethics, and long-term oral health.

Our Dental Services

We offer a wide range of dental treatments to keep your smile healthy and bright.

General Checkups

Comprehensive dental exams to maintain your oral health and catch issues early.

Root Canal

Painless root canal treatments to save infected or severely decayed teeth.

Fillings

Crowns & Bridges

Dental Implants

Permanent and natural-looking replacements for missing teeth.

Full Mouth Rehabilitation

Comprehensive treatment to rebuild and restore all your teeth.

Cleaning & Scaling

Tooth Extraction

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening treatments for an instantly brighter, more confident smile.

Braces & Orthodontics

Cosmetic Dentistry
